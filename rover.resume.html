Bio

My name's Kelly. I moved to Boston from San Francisco to get my Master's in Publishing & Writing at Emerson. I'm an avid walker and photographer, and I enjoy having a furry friend come along with me as I adventure the city and get to know the area. I work remotely for an online publication where I write and edit articles on all things "fandon." In my free time, I enjoy reading, collaging, crocheting, and drawing. I like to stay busy and I especially like the company of a furry friend.
I am also fluent in Italian and can get by with Spanish.

Pet Experience

Before moving to college I rescued Cisca, an elegant indoor cat in her middle age, and Tyler, a six month old Cockapoo, whom I trained. Once Tyler was well trained we added my neighbors sweet middle aged Frenchie Chihuahua mix, Henley, on our walks. While living in San Francisco I walked dozens of doggos over the years. I have always had a strong affinity to animals and have great respect for them, in all their individualities. I accept a level of parenting involved in handling animals and understand there are some pets with certain boundaries to be respected. I am very eager to get to know all the different furry friends that live in and around the city.
